Model Notes

  • The rubber band controls source order and which marks wrap behind the front turn.
  • The glass field stays fixed in display space and determines visible thickness and length.
  • Upper phase gets thicker from 3pm to 9am. Lower phase mirrors that and thickens from 9am to 3pm.
  • The seam is pinned at 3pm, and disappearing spokes leave only on the lower side of that seam.
